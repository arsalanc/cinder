# Player Guide

CINDER is two things at once: a **sandbox** for playing with a living element
simulation, and a **roguelite** descent through procedurally generated caves.

## Controls

**Sandbox (Create tab)**
- **Left-drag** — paint the selected element. Pick *Erase* to erase.
- **Scroll** — zoom toward the cursor. **Right-drag** — pan while zoomed.
- **`[` / `]`** — brush size. Zoomed in, the same brush paints fewer cells
  (finer placement).
- **1–9** — quick-select the first nine elements. **P** — pause.
- **Lightning** (palette tool) — click to call a real bolt at the cursor.
- **Generate** makes a new procedural map; **Weather** buttons force or
  silence rain/snow/storms; **Daily Run** / **Replay** at the bottom.

**Run (Play tab, or Enter)**
- **A / D** move, **W / Space** jump, **hold jump** airborne to jetpack
  (its own fuel bar; refuels on the ground or in water).
- **Left-click** casts toward the cursor; **right-click / wheel / 1–5**
  switch spells. **M** toggles full-map view; **R** respawns.

## How a run works

Descend to the pulsing purple **portal** on each level; clearing it offers a
choice of **1-of-3 synergies** before the next, deeper level. Reach and clear
**depth 6** to win. Death ends the run (your best depth is saved).

- **Essence shards** — the portal starts dormant. Each level scatters 2–4
  shards in far-apart pockets; collect them all to wake it. Digging straight
  to the exit gains nothing — the route *is* the level.
- **Bosses** guard depths 3 and 6 instead of shards, in a purpose-built
  **arena** (a walled chamber with a water reservoir). The portal opens when
  the boss dies (rewarding +30 HP and full mana). Bosses fight in **phases**
  (marked on their HP bar) and periodically **surface into a vulnerable
  window** — they glow gold, stop attacking, and take double damage. That's
  your cue to commit. The **Magma Worm** superheats the arena as it weakens,
  so the water reservoir becomes both its weakness (it sizzles in it) and
  your refuge from heatstroke. See the [Bestiary](bestiary.md).
- **Synergies** stack for the run and some are locked behind milestones
  (best depth, wins, kills). Full list in [Wand & Synergies](spells.md).
- **Dig Blast** is always in your loadout and mana regenerates, so you can
  never be permanently walled in.

## Surviving the world

The environment is as dangerous as the enemies — and it's the same simulation
you play with in the sandbox.

- **Fire & lava** set you *burning* (damage over time); **water** puts it out.
  **Acid** corrodes; **electrified water** shocks hard — don't swim near a
  storm, a live generator, or a Tempest.
- **Temperature** is a survival stat. Freezing biomes and cold snaps drain
  your **body warmth** toward hypothermia (being wet makes it worse); stand
  by fire or lava to warm up. Volcanic heat can cause heatstroke. Your spawn
  is always placed somewhere temperate — the danger is where you *go*.
- **Ice is slippery** — momentum carries and stops become skids.
- **Weather**: rain refills pools, cold snaps freeze them and bury the ground
  in snow (watch a meadow brown out and bloom again on the thaw), storms
  throw lightning that conducts through water and metal.

## Learn the interactions

The depth of CINDER is in how elements combine — burning oil slicks on water,
electrolyzing a pool into explosive hydrogen, wiring a lava-heated metal rod
into a lake, fusing sand into glass. The complete, always-current reference:
